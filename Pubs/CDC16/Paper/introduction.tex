\textbf{Notation}.
Given two subsets $A,B$ of $\Re^n$, define their \textit{Minkowski sum} to be $A\oplus B \defeq \{a+b \such a\in A, b\in B\}$.
Define their Pontryagin difference to be $A\ominus B = \{c \in \Re^n \such c+b \in A \forall b \in B\}$
